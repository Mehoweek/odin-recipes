Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is famously known all around the world Italian dish, and it actually originates from... yeah, you guessed it. Italia. Who would've known. It's very time-consuming to make at home, but it's definitely worth the effort.

Ingredients:

  • pasta
  • tomato passata
  • minced meat of your choice
  • sour cream
  • basil
    •

    How to prepare:

    1. Make beshamel sauce
    2. Make layers of pasta and sauces
    3. Put it in the oven for around 40 minutes
