Tikka Masala

Description

Tikka Masala, for all lovers of very spicy food, it's a sin not to eat Tikka at least once a week! We can joke around about it, but it's actually not for everyone to eat, especially for more sensitive tongues.

Ingredients:

  • rice
  • chicken
  • sour cream
  • canned tomatoes
  • chili cayennne
    •

    How to prepare:

    1. Mix chicken breasts knifed to smal pieces with sour cream and spices
    2. Cook chicken on a pan
    3. Mix everything in a blender, toss it onto the chicken and cook rice
